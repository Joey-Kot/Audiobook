Third Party Notices

Audiobook-Studio uses the following third-party components:

1. FFmpeg/libav

Used by the application for audio decoding, resampling, MP3 encoding, and muxing.
Local fallback builds may also call a system-installed ffmpeg executable.

FFmpeg is a multimedia framework developed by the FFmpeg project.
FFmpeg is licensed under the GNU Lesser General Public License (LGPL) version 2.1 or later,
or the GNU General Public License (GPL) version 2 or later, depending on the build configuration.

This project builds and statically links a trimmed FFmpeg/libav configuration for release builds
on Windows, macOS, and Linux. The trimmed configuration is used only for audiobook audio
processing and currently enables decoding for common TTS outputs, resampling, and MP3 output.

No FFmpeg nonfree components are intentionally enabled.

https://ffmpeg.org/
